How Stellar Scout works

Transit Method

Detects planets by measuring the dimming of starlight when a planet passes in front of its host star. Most successful method used by Kepler and TESS missions.

üì°

Radial Velocity

Measures the wobble of a star caused by the gravitational pull of orbiting planets. Detects the Doppler shift in the star's spectrum.

AI Detection

Machine learning models analyze light curves and stellar parameters to identify exoplanet signals, reducing false positives and accelerating discovery.
